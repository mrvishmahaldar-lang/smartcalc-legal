# Privacy Policy for SmartCalc

Effective Date: 01 Jan 2026

SmartCalc ("we", "our", "us") is a financial calculator application
designed for educational and estimation purposes only.

## Information Collection
We do not collect or store personal financial data entered into the app.

## Third-Party Services
SmartCalc uses the following third-party services:
- Google AdMob (for advertisements)
- Firebase Analytics (for app usage analytics)

These services may collect anonymous usage data in accordance with
their respective privacy policies.

## Data Usage
- No income, tax, or financial calculations are stored or transmitted.
- All calculations are performed locally on the user's device.

## Security
We take reasonable measures to protect user data. However, no method
of electronic storage is 100% secure.

## Children's Information
SmartCalc does not knowingly collect personal data from children
under the age of 13.

## Changes
This policy may be updated from time to time.
